The Best Movies According TO Lidya

The top 3 movies of all time

Boys over flowers

Poor girl attends the elite Shin Hwa High and is bullied by the leader of F4 (the four richest boys). He becomes attracted to her; however, she has a crush on his best friend. Whom will she choose? Jan Di, the Heroine, is the poor but bright girl who has a strong sense of justice and full of vitality.

Kuch Kuch Hotahe

The plot combines two love triangles set years apart. The first half covers friends on a college campus, while the second tells the story of a widower's young daughter who tries to reunite her dad with his old best friend.

Iliski Durumu Karisik

Aysegul, an author who works from home, suddenly finds herself on vacation in Greece with a surprise trip that has been arranged friends. While on the trip which was financed by her friends, Aysegul meets the famous series actor Can Tekin. After a short while she realizes that things are not what they seems to be.
